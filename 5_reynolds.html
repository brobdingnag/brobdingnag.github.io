Reynoldsの輸送定理(3パターン)を証明する。 \begin{align} \Dif{}{t}\int_{B_t} \phi(\Vec{x},t) \diff V &= \int_{B_t}\left(\Dif{\phi(\Vec{x},t)}{t} + \phi(\Vec{x},t)\nabla \cdot \Vec{v}\right)\diff V \\ &= \int_{B_t}\pdif{\phi(\Vec{x},t)}{t} \diff V + \int_{\partial B_t} \phi(\Vec{x},t) \Vec{v} \cdot \Vec{n}\diff S \\ &= \int_{B_t}\left(\pdif{\phi(\Vec{x},t)}{t} + \nabla \cdot ( \phi(\Vec{x},t) \Vec{v} ) \right)\diff V \\ \end{align} 一行目の証明は以下のようになる。 \begin{align} \Dif{}{t}\int_{B_t} \phi(\Vec{x},t) \diff V &=\Dif{}{t}\int_{B_0} \hat{\phi}(\Vec{X},t) J\diff V_0 \\ &=\int_{B_0}\Dif{}{t} (\hat{\phi}(\Vec{X},t) J)\diff V_0 \\ &=\int_{B_0}\left( J\Dif{\hat{\phi}(\Vec{X},t)}{t} + \hat{\phi}(\Vec{X},t)\Dif{J}{t} \right)\diff V_0 \\ &=\int_{B_0}\left( \Dif{\hat{\phi}(\Vec{X},t)}{t} + \hat{\phi}(\Vec{X},t)\nabla \cdot \Vec{v} \right)J\diff V_0 \\ &=\int_{B_t}\left( \Dif{\phi(\Vec{x},t)}{t} + \phi(\Vec{x},t)\nabla \cdot \Vec{v} \right)\diff V \\ \end{align} 二行目の証明は以下のようになる。 \begin{align} \int_{B_t}\left( \Dif{\phi(\Vec{x},t)}{t} + \phi(\Vec{x},t)\nabla \cdot \Vec{v} \right)\diff V &= \int_{B_t}\left( \pdif{\phi(\Vec{x},t)}{t} + \Vec{v} \cdot \nabla \phi(\Vec{x},t) +\phi(\Vec{x},t)\nabla \cdot \Vec{v} \right)\diff V\\ &=\int_{B_t}\left( \pdif{\phi(\Vec{x},t)}{t}+\nabla\cdot(\phi(\Vec{x},t) \Vec{v}) \right)\diff V\\ &=\int_{B_t}\pdif{\phi(\Vec{x},t)}{t} \diff V + \int_{\partial B_t} \phi(\Vec{x},t) \Vec{v} \cdot \Vec{n}\diff S \\ \end{align} 三行目の証明は以下のようになる。 \begin{align} \int_{B_t}\pdif{\phi(\Vec{x},t)}{t} \diff V + \int_{\partial B_t} \phi(\Vec{x},t) \Vec{v} \cdot \Vec{n}\diff S &= \int_{B_t}\pdif{\phi(\Vec{x},t)}{t} \diff V +\int_{\partial B_t} \phi(\Vec{x},t) v_i n_i\diff S \\ &= \int_{B_t}\pdif{\phi(\Vec{x},t)}{t} \diff V +\int_{B_t} \pdif{\phi(\Vec{x},t)v_i}{x_i}\diff V \\ &= \int_{B_t}\left(\pdif{\phi(\Vec{x},t)}{t} + \nabla \cdot (\phi(\Vec{x},t)\Vec{v})\right)\diff V \\ \end{align} ただし途中でヤコビアンの物質微分 \begin{align} \Dif{J}{t} = J\nabla \cdot \Vec{v} \end{align} ベクトル解析の公式 \begin{align} \nabla \cdot (\phi\Vec{v}) &= \pdif{}{x_i}(\phi v_i) \\ &=v_i \pdif{\phi}{x_i}+\phi\pdif{v_i}{x_i} \\ &=\Vec{v} \cdot \nabla \phi + \phi\nabla \cdot \Vec{v} \end{align} を用いた。
